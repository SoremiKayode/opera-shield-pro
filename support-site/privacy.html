Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Data handling principles

  • Opera Shield Pro is built to minimize data collection.
  • The extension does not require account creation.
  • Configuration preferences are stored locally in browser extension storage.

What data may be processed

The extension may inspect network request metadata needed to apply blocking and filtering rules. This processing is used for functionality and is not sold as personal data.

Support contact

If you need deletion/export help or have privacy questions, email support@operashieldpro.app.

Policy updates

We may update this policy as functionality or legal requirements evolve. Material updates will be reflected on this page with a revised date.
