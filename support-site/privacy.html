Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Overview

Shield Pro Plugins is designed with a privacy-first model. We aim to process only the minimum data needed for ad/tracker filtering functionality and product support.

Information we collect

  • Extension settings: User preferences are stored in browser extension storage for local operation.
  • Support communication: If you contact us, we process the information you include in your message to respond and resolve your issue.
  • Operational request context: The extension may evaluate request metadata in-browser to apply filtering rules.

Information we do not sell

We do not sell personal data. We do not require account registration to use core extension features.

Data retention

Support emails are retained only as needed for issue handling, auditing, and legal compliance. Local extension settings remain under user browser control.

Security and governance

We apply practical safeguards and review extension behavior as features evolve. Policy updates are published on this page with a revised date.

Company information

Shield Pro Plugins is developed by CodeIgnite, a company that builds sophisticated SaaS programs and privacy-focused software products.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, email support@operashieldpro.app.
